  Score Range  | Interpretation        | Action
  ─────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
  75-100       | 🔥 VIRAL             | Capitalize on momentum!
  50-74        | 👍 GOOD              | Solid engagement
  1-49         | 📊 MODERATE          | Room for growth
  0            | 😴 LOW               | Boost visibility

  How it's calculated:
  • Volume (40%):     Total mentions vs expected
  • Sentiment (30%):  Polarity score (-1 to +1)
  • Engagement (30%): Concentration during event
